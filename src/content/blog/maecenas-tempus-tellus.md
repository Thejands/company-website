# Turning Vague Requirements into Clear Scope

Ambiguous requests are normal at the start of enterprise work. What matters is how quickly the team converts ambiguity into decisions that can be estimated and delivered.

## Steps to Success

1. **Define the business outcome** before listing features.
2. **Separate must-haves from optional asks** in writing.
3. **Agree acceptance criteria** so sign-off is objective.

Once scope is structured this way, teams can move with confidence: engineering can estimate realistically, product can prioritize clearly, and leadership can track progress without interpretation gaps.
