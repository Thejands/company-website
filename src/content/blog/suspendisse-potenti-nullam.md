# Simplifying Complex Product Architecture

Complexity is unavoidable in enterprise products, but confusion is optional. The best architecture choices simplify decisions for both developers and stakeholders.

> "Good architecture reduces decision fatigue for both engineers and stakeholders."

## Design Principles

Use clear boundaries between modules, document ownership, and define escalation paths early. Teams move faster when they know who decides what, what "done" means, and how quality is measured before launch.
