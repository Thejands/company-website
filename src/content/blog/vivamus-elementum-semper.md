# Designing Dashboards That Drive Decisions

Dashboards become useful when each metric is tied to an action. Without that link, teams spend time reading charts but still struggle to decide what to do next.

Start with decisions first: which signals indicate healthy performance, where intervention is required, and how quickly a team can respond when a threshold is crossed.

## Data Visualization

Good visualization then becomes straightforward: fewer vanity metrics, clearer trend context, and role-specific views for product, operations, and leadership.
